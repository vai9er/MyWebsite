Music

As a Toronto-based jazz musician, I have been honing my skills on the saxophone for over 10 years. I learned to play in Boca Raton, Florida, and during my time in the U.S I was accepted into the prestigious Palm Beach All-District Band and later invited to participate in the annual Spotlight on Young Musicians along with contributing towards high MPA ratings in 2015 and 2016 representing Loggers' Run Middle School

From 2016-2018, I participated in Humber College's Youth Jazz Program, where I competed in MusicFest Canada 2017, advancing to the National Stage round and receiving an Honour Award for outstanding performance

From 2018-2020, I performed as a Saxophone and Clarinet Doublist for the Etobicoke Jazz Band. In 2019-2020, I performed as the lead Alto Saxophonist for 91.1 FM's Youth Big Band

I now play at various gigs in Toronto while trying to balance a Computer Science degree at the University of Toronto.
